The Game

You are taking a trip on the Coast Starlight train. There is a murder. Solve the murder!

How to win

You have a certain number of likeability points at the beginning. As you interact with other characters and everyone tries to solve the mystery, you can gain or lose likeability points. You must solve the mystery before your likeability points get too low with all players or they will decide you are the murderer and literally throw you off the train TO DEATH.
